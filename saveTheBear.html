Save The Bear


Challenge

Volunteers, Advocates, Hikers want to be able to submit the whereabouts or locations of bears that are deemed to be endangered

Solution

Create a tool that will allow users to report sightings of a bear which will be loaded into the database. Also having the ability to pull up a report of bear sightings and their locations

Target Users

  • Casual hikers that encounter bears.
  • Advocates and Volunteers who are out in the field.
  • Managers, CEO's who are in the office.

Challenges and Constraints

  • Having to navigate easily.
  • Being able to quickly start the Camera app to take a picture and fill out the form easily.
  • Being able to know what type of bear you are looking at.
  • Pull up reportrs.
