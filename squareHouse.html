Square House Mobile App


Challenge

A mobile application for a local favorite restaurant, that customers can order from and expand their reach.

Solution

A mobile application that allows for users to order food and customize it to their preference.

Target Users

  • Regular and local attendees to the restaurants.
  • Those who are not able to come in and have a meal, but can only do delivery because of time constraints.

Challenges and Constraints

  • Having an efficient user flow to order food.
  • Having accessibility options for those who are older.
  • An appealing and effective layout.
